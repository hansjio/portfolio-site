About

"I write code the way I shoot film — deliberately, frame by frame, knowing that what's left out matters as much as what's in."

Aspiring Software engineer and data scientist based in Manila, Philippines, drawn to problems at the intersection of systems thinking and statistical reasoning. I've spent the last several years building data pipelines, ML models, and backend infrastructure that — when things go right — quietly disappear into the product and just work.

I care deeply about craft, legibility, and the kind of engineering that holds up long after the sprint ends. When I'm not in front of a terminal, I'm loading a camera.
